About

Hello! My name is Caleb Ballard. I'm a Systems Test Engineer at NASA Johnson Space Center under KBR. Currently working in the International Space Station (ISS), Orion, Gateway, and Starliner space programs. I have experience in design software such as MATLAB and AutoCAD and experience with coding languages such as C, C++, Python.

During my tenure as a Systems Test Engineer at KBR Inc., I collaborated closely with interdisciplinary teams and external partners, including NASA, to develop comprehensive test plans and verify the functionality of complex systems. This experience equipped me with the ability to thrive in fast-paced environments while ensuring the highest standards of quality and reliability. My technical proficiency in programming languages such as C++, C, Python, and a little bit of C# coupled with my familiarity with application software like Linux and MATLAB, positions me well to tackle the challenges associated with developing cutting-edge financial software solutions. Moreover, my leadership roles within professional organizations such as the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA-UH) and Space City Rocketry have honed my communication, teamwork, and project management skills. As Vice President of AIAA-UH, I successfully managed the organization in the absence of the President and spearheaded recruitment activities to foster a vibrant community of aerospace enthusiasts. Similarly, as Avionics Sub-Team Lead at Space City Rocketry, I led a team in the development of avionics systems, ensuring timely completion and adherence to project goals.
